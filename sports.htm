SPORTS

AS PLAYER :
  1. Represented Assam Junior Volleyball Team in 1975 - 1976
  2. Selected as the Stand-By player Assam Senior Volleyball Team in 1973
  3. Captain of the Volleyball team, University Classes and got the Best Volleyball Player prize of 1977-78
  4. Played for the Cricket team of University Classes in the years 1977- 1978
  5. Captain of Cotton College Teachers’ Cricket team for five year
  6. Awarded Man of the Tournament in the S. N. Hazarika Inter College Teachers’ Cricket Tournament org. by Assam Agricultural University, in 1990

SPORTS MANAGEMENT BODIES :
  1. Member- Board of Sports of Assam, Govt. of Assam for two terms 1986-1992
  2. Member- State Sports Council, Govt. of Assam from 1986 and 1989
  3. Vice President- State Sports Council, Govt. of Assam for three years since June 1990
  4. Member- Gauhati University Sports Board for the years 1986-87

AS SPORTS ORGANISER :
  1. As Organising Secretary organised the Inter College Sports Meet of Gauhati University, held in Nowgong College in 1986
  2. Organising Cotton College Annual Sports meets since 1987 being Prof. in-charge of any one of the sports sections or as Vice President of Athletic Section of the college
  3. Organised Guwahati Traditional Sports Festival in Latasil ground, as Organizing Secretary of ‘Sishupran Nabin Sarma’ Centenary celebration, 2000
