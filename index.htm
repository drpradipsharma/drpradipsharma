Dr Pradip Sharma is a man of multifarious activities, known for his teaching, speeches, organizing abilities, research and paintings. Dr Sharma not only nurtures the young talents to reach the fullest potential but also inspires people of all ages through his popular radio talks and public speeches. Handling of large number of projects, consultative works and memberships in many govt. advisory and technical bodies made him resourceful to the young researchers. In addition, his valuable paintings and portraits give lives to the walls of many homes and auditoriums.

Dr Pradip Sharma, retired as an Associate Professor in Geography, Cotton University, started his teaching career in the year 1978 in Nowgong College. In 1987 he has been appointed in Cotton College and then onwards besides teaching he is shouldering many responsibilities in the college and later in the Cotton University.

He originally belongs to Nagaon and spent his childhood on the bank of the river Kalong. He is the second son of Late Dharmeswar Sarma and Late Nilima Devi. He started his school career in Nowgong Montessori School and studied up to HS level in Nowgong Govt. Boys’ HS School. He took Bachelor Degree (Honours in Geography) with first class from Cotton College and took Masters, M Phil and Ph D from Gauhati University.
